The Bigger Picture

The farm is sited in a location where natural earthen fish ponds not only thrive but provide a natural home for fish’s elevated life. Fishes of different varieties come in harmony with nature and in appreciation of comfortable living conditions provide high yields. Will you measure your height with the length of a fish? It can be fun doing that. After all, you are the one to hold the fish and measure, in this act the fish has no say. At the farm, you can even do more than just sightseeing. The choice of what you enjoy on the farm depends on your fancy. Come prepared as our team of staff are on the prowl to give you an unforgettable experience. The Gold Talent Farm’s location is itself a product of an expedition. As we went in search of what can happen and how the idle mass land situated in the rural marshy but serene environment of the ancient city can be usefully exploited, that is how the site brought itself to the fore.

And thankfully, the plots were naturally prepared primarily for a fish farm venture. The beautiful fish pond layout is yet another mud-architectural display, just very nice to behold. The hustle and bustle activities of the fish are welcoming. And the fishes’ stridulations can be spiritedly felt as their welcome songs to visitors.
