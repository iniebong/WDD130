Carnival Calabar

Have you ever watched the CALABAR CARNIVAL live? If no, then you have missed a lot. Calabar Carnival is a yearly fun-filled festival beamed in a captivating street dance party, laced with assorted themes of the rich African cultural fragrance and championed by the energetic beautiful and handsome youths. Dressed in a variety of costumes designed not only satisfy the admiration of the viewers, but intended to raise their curiosity of those with the devils’ prying eyes. Visiting our site will give you the links and more information above the Carnival-Calabar and more. The farm is also a school. It offers farms’ management and production training and aquatic skills development for interested people. In the farm you are trained not only on hatchery, brooding, management and how to source your market, but also on how to manufacture you animal rations just to achieve your maximum desired farm yields. It as well trains its applicants on digital farming and digital marketing of their products.
